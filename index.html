The World Inside Our Home

Imagine this is your home.

How many things inside it do you know where it came from? How much do you know about it? For many of us, the things in our home are, in many ways, mysterious to us.

We’re here to unfurl some of the mysteries rolled into the stories of our dependency on places and people around the world.

We explore which countries you depend on for the things you use at home. We also look at what common things at home reveal about the world we live in.

The Food We Eat, Headline Here

The UK imported food from 181 countries and territories in 2017. Six EU countries—Netherlands, Ireland, France, Germany, Spain, and Italy—were suppliers of already half of the total import bill of £43.52 billion. Wine, broths, ketchup, sauces, condiments, pastry, chocolate, cheese, and chicken were the top import into the UK.
