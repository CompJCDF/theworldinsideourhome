The World Inside Our Home

Around 50% of all food consumed in the UK are imported from a total of

206

countries and territories in the last decade. Wine, ketchup and sauces, and other condiments, pastry, chocolate, cheese, meat, bananas, grapes, and tomatoes are the UK’s most valuable food import. These products totalled more than £21.9 billion, or 50% of the total £43.52 billion import amount in 2017.
